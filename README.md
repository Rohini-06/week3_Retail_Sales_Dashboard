# Retail Sales Dashboard (2023–2024)

## Project Overview
This project was developed as part of Mission 3: Data Visualization & Dashboards under the VertexMind Data Analytics Internship. The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data and create an interactive dashboard that provides meaningful business insights through effective data visualization.

## Interactive Dashboard
🔗 [View Dashboard]((https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/4ef2a74d-8a10-49f7-be5a-8da84290083a))

## Tools Used
- Google Looker Studio
- Microsoft Excel

## Dataset
The dataset contains retail sales transactions, including customer demographics, product categories, quantities sold, dates, and total sales amounts.

## Business Questions
The dashboard addresses the following business questions:

1. Which product category sold the most and the least?
2. Which age group purchases the most products?
3. What is the total revenue generated each month?
4. What is the total quantity of products sold?
5. Which gender contributes the highest sales?

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards for Total Transactions, Total Revenue, and Total Quantity Sold
- Product-wise Quantity Sold Analysis
- Product Sales Distribution
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Sales Distribution by Gender
- Purchase Analysis by Age Group
- Interactive Filters for better exploration of data

## Key Insights
- Clothing is the highest-selling product category.
- Beauty is the least-selling product category.
- Customers aged 46–55 years purchase the most products.
- The business generated approximately ₹456K in total revenue.
- A total of 2,514 units were sold.
- Female customers contribute slightly higher sales compared to male customers.

## Conclusion
The Retail Sales Dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights. It helps understand customer behavior, monitor sales performance, and support data-driven business decisions through interactive and visually appealing charts.
